Your Keys, Your Fees.

R3FI is a yield generating, non-custodial (your tokens always stay in your wallet) ERC-20 token that requires no lockup and no gas to claim your rewards. Whether your tokens are stored in MetaMask, in your Hardware wallet or a titanium plate under your house foundations, they receive fees from each transaction.

Instant rewards from the second your wallet holds R3FI.

A 5% transaction fee is distributed amongst token holders from Every. Single. Transaction as it happens.

Decentralised and trustless through and through.

DeFi all the way, the contract is owned by no-one, immutable and completely autonomous. Even the community is decentralised, with no expensive team calling the shots. There was no huge ICO with massive team allocation. Every single contributor purchased their own tokens.
